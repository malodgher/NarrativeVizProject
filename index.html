COVID-19 cases in the U.S. vs. COVID-19 cases in U.S. states and territories

The two charts below show:

  • A line graph of the total number of COVID-19 cases in the United States each day since January 21, 2020
  • A bar chart of the rate of increase of COVID-19 cases in the United States each day since January 21, 2020

Hover over the line chart to see how many total cases were reported on a specific day. Hover over a bar in the bar chart to see, for a given day, what the increase in the number of cases were compared to the previous day. You can also use you mouse to zoom in on an area in the bar chart




Use the dropdown menu below to select a U.S. state or terriory. The following charts will then show:

  • A line graph of the total number of COVID-19 cases in the selected state/territory each day since the first recoded date of COVID-19 cases in that state/territory
  • A bar chart of the rate of increase of COVID-19 cases in the selected state/territory each day since since the first recoded date of COVID-19 cases in that state/territory

The controls in the charts above also apply to the charts below. You can use the dropdown to change the selected state/territory, which will change the data shown in the charts below
